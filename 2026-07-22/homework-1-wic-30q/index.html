Question 1

Moderate

A historian reconstructing a coastal trading network has only three surviving years of harbor records. The ships named in those records match routes mentioned in private letters, but gaps in both archives prevent the historian from claiming that the pattern held throughout the century. She therefore presents her reconstruction as ______.

Which choice completes the text with the most logical and precise word or phrase?

  1. A. exhaustive
  2. B. provisional
  3. C. arbitrary
  4. D. immutable
